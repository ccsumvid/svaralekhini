🚀 Getting Started

  1. Allow microphone access when prompted
  2. Select your base pitch (Sa) and notation style
  3. Choose your preferred language
  4. Click "Play Drone" to hear reference pitch
  5. Enter lyrics in the text area
  6. Click "Start Recording" and sing

🎼 Carnatic Notation

  • Svaras: Sa, Ri₁, Ri₂, Ga₁, Ga₂, Ma₁, Ma₂, Pa, Dha₁, Dha₂, Ni₁, Ni₂
  • Duration: Sa (short), Sa_ (medium), Sa__ (long)
  • Glides: Sa~ (gamaka/pitch bend)
