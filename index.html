üöÄ Getting Started

  1. Allow microphone access when prompted
  2. Choose your notation style (Western or Carnatic)
  3. Select your shruti (base pitch Sa) from C3 to B4 range
  4. Choose your preferred language for lyrics
  5. Click "Play" button to hear your selected shruti
  6. Enter lyrics in the text area
  7. Click "Start Recording" and sing along
  8. Click "Stop Recording" when finished
  9. Click "Analyze" to see your notation results
